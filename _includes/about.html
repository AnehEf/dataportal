About

The WSLH Data Portal allows users to explore information made available from public datasets. Here, data from different WSLH programs and projects are collected and organized in a central location to provide accessible up to date information about our community. Data is transformed, linked, and visualized to increase clarity, reveal trends, simplify access, and promote overall information transparency.

The WSLH Data Portal includes both data generated by the WSLH and its public health partners. Data is made publicly available after review and is compliant with Data-Sharing and Usage agreements. As more data becomes available new dashboards will continue to be added.
